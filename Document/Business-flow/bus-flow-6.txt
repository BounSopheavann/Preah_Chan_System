BUSINESS FLOW 6 – FOLLOW-UP, RECALL & TELEGRAM PATIENT PORTAL

You are working on a Dental Clinic Management System.

This document is the official explanation of Business Flow 6.

Treat this document as the source of truth.

This document explains how the clinic manages patient follow-up
appointments, long-term recall reminders, and Telegram Mini App
integration after a patient leaves the clinic.

Future reminder services, Telegram integration, analytics, dashboards,
and patient engagement features should follow this document.

============================================================ MODULE
PURPOSE ============================================================

Business Flow 6 begins after billing has been completed.

Payment Completed ↓ Determine Next Action ↓ Follow-up Required? ↓ Create
Follow-up Appointment ↓ OR Create Recall Reminder ↓ Notify Patient ↓
Patient Returns (Business Flow 2)

Goal

• Keep patients returning for treatment. • Reduce missed follow-up
visits. • Improve long-term patient retention. • Provide patients with a
simple self-service portal.

============================================================ ACTORS
============================================================

Patient

Receptionist

Dentist

Administrator

System

Telegram Mini App

============================================================
PRE-CONDITIONS
============================================================

• Billing workflow is complete. • Appointment status is Completed. •
Patient profile exists.

============================================================ WORKFLOW
============================================================

STEP 1 DETERMINE NEXT ACTION

Business Action

Dentist decides whether the patient needs:

• Follow-up visit • Additional treatment • Routine recall • No further
appointment

System Response

Display treatment outcome options.

============================================================

STEP 2 CREATE FOLLOW-UP

Decision

Follow-up required?

YES

Business Action

Receptionist schedules the next appointment.

Examples

• Crown fitting

• Root canal continuation

• Suture removal

• Orthodontic review

System Response

Create new appointment.

Link to existing patient.

============================================================

STEP 3 CREATE RECALL

Decision

Routine recall required?

YES

Business Action

Receptionist or system schedules future recall.

Examples

• 6-month cleaning

• Annual examination

• Preventive check-up

System Response

Create recall record.

Store:

• Due date

• Reminder status

• Recall reason

============================================================

STEP 4 SEND REMINDERS

System Response

Automatically send reminders through:

• Telegram

• SMS

• Email

Reminder timing may include:

• 24 hours before appointment

• 2 hours before appointment

• Recall due date

Track reminder status:

• Sent

• Delivered

• Read (future)

• Failed

============================================================

STEP 5 TELEGRAM MINI APP

Business Action

Receptionist links patient’s Telegram account using a secure QR code or
invitation token.

System Response

Create Telegram link.

Patient may view:

• Upcoming appointments

• Treatment progress

• Outstanding balance

• Treatment history

• Recall reminders

• Clinic announcements (future)

============================================================

STEP 6 PATIENT RESPONSE

Decision

Patient books appointment?

YES

→ Return to Business Flow 2.

NO

→ Continue monitoring recall schedule.

System Response

Update reminder outcome.

Examples

• Booked

• Ignored

• Declined

============================================================ VALIDATION
RULES ============================================================

• Every recall belongs to one patient.

• Every follow-up appointment belongs to one patient.

• Telegram account may only link to one patient profile.

• Reminder dates cannot occur before today’s date.

• Duplicate recall reminders should be prevented.

============================================================ BUSINESS
RULES ============================================================

1.  Every patient should leave with one of three outcomes:

• Treatment completed

• Follow-up appointment booked

• Recall reminder created

2.  Follow-up appointments have higher priority than routine recalls.

3.  Telegram linking should be performed by staff using a secure QR
    code.

4.  Returning patients always reuse the existing patient profile.

============================================================ OUTPUTS
============================================================

• Follow-up appointment

• Recall record

• Reminder history

• Telegram account link

• Updated patient engagement history

============================================================ EXCEPTION
FLOWS ============================================================

Patient declines follow-up

→ Record refusal.

Reminder delivery fails

→ Retry using another communication method.

Telegram linking fails

→ Generate a new secure invitation token.

Patient changes phone number

→ Update profile before sending future reminders.

============================================================
POST-CONDITIONS
============================================================

• Patient has an appropriate next action.

• Reminder schedule is active.

• Telegram portal is linked if available.

• Clinic can continue long-term patient tracking.
