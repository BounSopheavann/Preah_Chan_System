# ERD 1 – Patient Management & Appointment System

You are working on a Dental Clinic Management System.

This document is the official explanation of ERD 1.

Treat this document as the source of truth.

------------------------------------------------------------
MODULE PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

This module manages the first part of the clinic workflow.

Patient Registration
        ↓
Patient Profile
        ↓
Appointment Booking
        ↓
Appointment Scheduling

It allows receptionists to register patients, create appointments, assign dentists, and manage appointment statuses.

This module is the entry point of the entire system.

------------------------------------------------------------
TABLES
------------------------------------------------------------

This ERD contains four tables.

1. users
2. patients
3. appointment_types

------------------------------------------------------------
BUSINESS RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 1

One patient must only have one patient profile.

Never create duplicate patients.

Search by phone first.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 2

Every appointment must belong to exactly one patient.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 3

Every appointment must have one appointment type.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 4

Every appointment must have one assigned dentist.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 5

Receptionist creates appointments.

Dentists do not create appointments during normal workflow.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 6

Returning patients are never re-registered.

Reuse the existing patient profile.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 7

Appointment status lifecycle

Booked

↓

Confirmed

↓

Checked-in

↓

In Chair

↓

Completed

Alternative

Booked

↓

Cancelled

or

Booked

↓

No-show

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 8

Deleting appointments is not recommended.

Instead

change status to

Cancelled

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 9

Every patient profile records allergies, medications, medical conditions, and consent status before treatment.
