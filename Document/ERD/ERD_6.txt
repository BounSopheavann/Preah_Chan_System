# ERD 6 – Inventory Management & Patient Recall

You are working on a Dental Clinic Management System.

This document is the official explanation of ERD 6.

Treat this document as the source of truth.

------------------------------------------------------------
MODULE PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

This module manages clinic inventory and patient follow-up.

It has two independent functions.

Inventory Workflow

Supplier
        ↓
Purchase Order
        ↓
Purchase Order Items
        ↓
Products
        ↓
Stock Movement

Recall Workflow

Patient
        ↓
Recall Schedule
        ↓
Reminder
        ↓
Patient Response

The purpose of this module is to ensure the clinic always has enough supplies while automatically reminding patients about future visits.

------------------------------------------------------------
BUSINESS RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 1

Every product belongs to the inventory master list.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 2

Products should never be deleted once they have stock history.

Mark them Inactive instead.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 3

Receiving a purchase order automatically creates Stock In movements.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 4

Completing a treatment may automatically create Stock Out movements if inventory tracking is enabled.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 5

Current stock is calculated from stock movements.

It should not rely only on manual updates.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 6

When current stock reaches the minimum stock level, the system should notify administrators.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 7

One patient may have multiple recalls throughout their lifetime.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 8

A recall may generate multiple reminder attempts before the patient responds.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 9

Completing a recall usually results in creating a new appointment.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 10

Telegram reminders should use the patient's linked Telegram account from ERD 2 whenever available.
