# ERD 4 – Treatment Execution & Clinical Records

You are working on a Dental Clinic Management System.

This document is the official explanation of ERD 4.

Treat this document as the source of truth.

------------------------------------------------------------
MODULE PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

This module manages the actual dental treatment after the treatment plan has been approved.

Workflow

Treatment Plan Item
        ↓
Treatment Execution
        ↓
Treatment Files
        ↓
Prescription

The purpose of this module is to record every completed dental procedure, attach supporting clinical files, and issue prescriptions when necessary.

------------------------------------------------------------
TABLES
------------------------------------------------------------

This ERD contains four tables.

1. procedures
2. treatment_execution
3. treatment_files
4. prescriptions

------------------------------------------------------------
BUSINESS RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 1

Treatment cannot begin without an appointment.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 2

Every treatment execution must belong to one appointment.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 3

Every treatment execution should originate from one treatment plan item.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 4

Only dentists can perform treatment executions.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 5

Each treatment execution records the actual procedure performed, even if it differs from the original treatment plan.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 6

Clinical files belong to treatment executions, not directly to appointments.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 7

Multiple files may be attached to one treatment execution.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 8

One treatment execution may generate zero, one, or multiple prescriptions.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 9

Completed treatment executions become invoice items in ERD 5.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 10

Treatment execution records should never be deleted after billing.

If corrections are needed, create adjustment records instead of removing historical data.
