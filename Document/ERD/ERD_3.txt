# ERD 3 – Clinical Notes & Treatment Planning

You are working on a Dental Clinic Management System.

This document is the official explanation of ERD 3.

Treat this document as the source of truth.

------------------------------------------------------------
MODULE PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

This module manages the dentist's clinical examination and treatment planning.

Workflow

Appointment
        ↓
Clinical Examination
        ↓
Clinical Note
        ↓
Treatment Plan
        ↓
Treatment Plan Items

The purpose of this module is to record the patient's dental condition, diagnosis, and future treatment plan before treatment begins.

------------------------------------------------------------

------------------------------------------------------------

------------------------------------------------------------
BUSINESS RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 1

Every treatment plan belongs to exactly one appointment.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 2

Every appointment should have one clinical note before treatment begins.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 3

A treatment plan may contain one or many treatment plan items.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 4

Each treatment plan item represents one procedure on one tooth.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 5

Treatment plan items are independent.

Each item has its own status.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 6

Completing all treatment plan items automatically changes the treatment plan status to Completed.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 7

Treatment plans cannot be deleted after treatment has started.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 8

Only dentists are allowed to create or modify clinical notes and treatment plans.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 9

Treatment plan items become Treatment Execution records during actual treatment (ERD 4).

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 10

Treatment plans are estimates only.

Invoices are generated only after completed treatment executions in ERD 5.
