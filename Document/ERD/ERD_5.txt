# ERD 5 – Billing & Payment Management

You are working on a Dental Clinic Management System.

This document is the official explanation of ERD 5.

Treat this document as the source of truth.

------------------------------------------------------------
MODULE PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

This module manages billing, invoice generation, and payment collection.

Workflow

Completed Treatment
        ↓
Invoice
        ↓
Invoice Items
        ↓
Payment
        ↓
Outstanding Balance

The purpose of this module is to automatically generate invoices from completed treatments, record patient payments, and track outstanding balances.

------------------------------------------------------------

1. invoices

------------------------------------------------------------
BUSINESS RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 1

Invoices are automatically generated from completed treatment executions.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 2

Only completed treatments can appear on an invoice.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 3

One appointment generates only one invoice.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 4

Each treatment execution creates one invoice item.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 5

Patients may make one or multiple payments for the same invoice.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 6

Invoice balance is calculated as:

Balance = Grand Total - Total Payments

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 7

Invoice status changes automatically.

Pending
↓

Partially Paid
↓

Paid

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 8

Only administrators or receptionists can issue invoices and record payments.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 9

Invoices should never be deleted after payment.

If mistakes occur, issue a credit note or adjustment instead.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 10

Every payment must belong to one invoice.

Payments cannot exist independently.
