# ERD 2 – Telegram Integration & Patient Linking

You are working on a Dental Clinic Management System.

This document is the official explanation of ERD 2.

Treat this document as the source of truth.

------------------------------------------------------------
MODULE PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

This module securely links a patient's clinic record to their Telegram Mini App account.

Workflow

Patient Profile
        ↓
Patient Invite
        ↓
QR Code / Invite Token
        ↓
Telegram Login
        ↓
Telegram Account Linked

This module prevents duplicate patient records and allows patients to access their clinic information through Telegram.

------------------------------------------------------------

------------------------------------------------------------

1. patients
2. patient_invites
3. telegram_links

------------------------------------------------------------
BUSINESS RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 1

Every Telegram account must belong to exactly one patient.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 2

One patient can only have one active Telegram link.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 3

A patient may receive multiple invite tokens over time.

Old invite tokens should become Expired after use or expiration.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 4

Invite tokens must be unique.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 5

QR Codes should contain only the invite token.

Never expose patient information inside the QR code.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 6

If a patient loses access to Telegram, the old link should be marked Unlinked before creating a new one.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 7

Patients cannot self-register directly.

Reception staff create the patient profile first.

Then a QR Code or invite token is generated.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 8

Successful linking creates one record in telegram_links.

The invite record becomes Used.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 9

Expired or cancelled invitations cannot be reused.

------------------------------------------------------------

Rule 10

This module extends ERD 1.

Future modules use telegram_links to send:

• Appointment reminders

• Recall reminders

• Treatment progress

• Payment notifications

• Follow-up messages
