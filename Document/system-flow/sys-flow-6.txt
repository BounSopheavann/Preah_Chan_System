System Flow 6 Specification

Follow-up, Recall & Patient Discharge

This document defines System Flow 6 for the Dental Clinic Management
System.

System Flow 6 begins after the patient’s invoice has been finalized in
System Flow 5.

This is the final workflow in the patient’s visit cycle.

The flow ends when the patient leaves the clinic with all required
future actions recorded.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Primary Actors

-   Receptionist
-   Dentist

Supporting Actor

-   Patient

System

-   Dental Clinic Management System

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Scope

Included

-   Post-treatment instructions
-   Follow-up appointment
-   Recall scheduling
-   Reminder creation
-   Patient discharge
-   Telegram patient portal update
-   Visit completion

Excluded

-   Registration
-   Check-in
-   Treatment
-   Billing

These belong to previous system flows.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Overall Business Logic

Every patient should leave the clinic with one of the following
outcomes:

• No further treatment required. • Follow-up appointment booked. •
Recall reminder created. • Remaining treatment scheduled.

No patient should leave without a final appointment status.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Validation Rules

-   Appointment must be completed before discharge.
-   Follow-up appointment requires date and provider.
-   Recall date cannot be earlier than today.
-   Reminder cannot exist without follow-up or recall.
-   Patient portal updates only if Telegram account is linked.
-   Visit cannot close while billing remains unpaid if clinic policy
    requires payment first.

Diagram Rules

Use standard flowchart notation.

Oval: Start / End

Rectangle: System process

Diamond: Decision

Arrows: Normal workflow.

Every decision must include YES and NO branches.

The diagram should flow from top to bottom.

This is the final system flow of the Dental Clinic Management System.

After completion, the patient lifecycle returns to future appointment
booking or recall when the patient visits again.
