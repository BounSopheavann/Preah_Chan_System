System Flow 5 Specification

Billing, Payment & Invoice Processing

This document defines System Flow 5 for the Dental Clinic Management
System.

System Flow 5 begins immediately after the dentist completes treatment.

All completed procedures have already been recorded by System Flow 4.

This flow ends after payment is processed and the invoice reaches its
final status.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Primary Actors

-   Receptionist (Cashier)

Supporting Actors

-   Patient
-   Dentist (optional for discounts or pricing approval)

System

-   Dental Clinic Management System

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Scope

Included

-   Generate invoice
-   Load completed procedures
-   Calculate totals
-   Discount handling
-   Partial payment
-   Multiple payment methods
-   Outstanding balance
-   Receipt generation
-   Payment recording

Excluded

-   Follow-up appointment
-   Recall scheduling

Those belong to System Flow 6.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Overall Business Logic

The system automatically converts completed procedures into invoice
items.

Receptionists should never manually type treatment prices unless they
have permission.

Every payment must be recorded.

Every invoice must finish with a valid status.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Validation Rules

-   Invoice must contain at least one completed procedure.
-   Total must equal procedure prices minus discounts plus taxes.
-   Payment cannot exceed invoice total.
-   Every payment requires a payment method.
-   Discounts require authorization when configured.
-   Receipt generated only after invoice creation.

System Flow 5 ends after invoice processing is complete and before
follow-up or recall scheduling begins.

System Flow 6 starts with post-treatment follow-up and recall
management.
