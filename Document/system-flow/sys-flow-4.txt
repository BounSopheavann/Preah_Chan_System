System Flow 4 Specification

Treatment Execution & Clinical Procedure

This document defines System Flow 4 for the Dental Clinic Management
System.

System Flow 4 begins immediately after the dentist and patient agree to
start treatment.

The patient is already in the dental chair and a treatment plan already
exists.

This flow ends when all procedures for the current visit are completed
or temporarily stopped.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Primary Actors

-   Dentist
-   Dental Assistant

Supporting Actor

-   Patient

System

-   Dental Clinic Management System

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Scope

Included

-   Begin treatment
-   Execute procedures
-   Record procedure details
-   Update odontogram
-   Record anesthesia
-   Record materials and consumables
-   Attach photos/X-rays
-   Clinical notes
-   Procedure completion
-   Partial treatment
-   Emergency interruption

Excluded

-   Invoice generation
-   Payment
-   Recall
-   Follow-up scheduling

Those belong to later system flows.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Overall Business Logic

The dentist performs clinical work while the system records everything
performed during the visit.

Every completed procedure must become part of the permanent patient
history.

The system should automatically prepare billing items from completed
procedures.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Validation Rules

-   Treatment cannot start without an approved treatment plan.
-   Every completed procedure must be linked to a tooth (if applicable).
-   Clinical notes required before finishing visit.
-   Procedure must have a responsible dentist.
-   Images belong to the current visit only.
-   Completed procedures become billable items.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Decisions

1.  Treatment plan exists?
2.  Anesthesia used?
3.  Consumables used?
4.  Need treatment images?
5.  Procedure completed?
6.  Treatment interrupted?
7.  More procedures today?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Diagram Rules

Use standard flowchart notation.

Oval: Start / End

Rectangle: System process

Diamond: Decision

Arrows: Normal workflow.

Every decision contains YES and NO branches.

The diagram should flow vertically with minimal crossing lines.

System Flow 4 ends after clinical treatment is finished and before
invoice generation begins.

System Flow 5 starts with billing and payment.
