System Flow 3 Specification

Clinical Examination, Diagnosis & Treatment Planning

This document defines System Flow 3 for the Dental Clinic Management
System.

This flow begins when the patient has already completed reception
check-in and is called into the treatment room.

This flow ends when the dentist finishes the examination, records the
diagnosis, creates the treatment plan, and decides whether treatment
will begin immediately or be scheduled for another visit.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Primary Actors

-   Dentist
-   Dental Assistant (optional)

Supporting Actor

-   Patient

System

-   Dental Clinic Management System

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Scope

Included

-   Patient enters treatment room
-   Open patient record
-   Review medical history
-   Review previous treatments
-   Clinical examination
-   Record findings
-   Diagnosis
-   Tooth chart (Odontogram)
-   X-ray / image attachment
-   Treatment plan creation
-   Cost estimation
-   Patient approval
-   Decision to start treatment now or later

Excluded

-   Actual treatment procedure
-   Billing
-   Payment
-   Follow-up

Those belong to later System Flows.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Overall Business Logic

The receptionist no longer participates.

The dentist becomes the primary user.

The system acts as an electronic dental record (EDR), displaying patient
history while allowing the dentist to document today’s visit.

Nothing should be handwritten outside the system.

Every examination should result in one of these outcomes:

• Treatment starts today. • Future appointment is required. • Patient
declines treatment.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Validation Rules

-   Dentist must be assigned.
-   Patient must be checked in.
-   Clinical note cannot be empty.
-   Diagnosis required before treatment planning.
-   Every treatment plan must contain at least one procedure.
-   Images must be linked to the current visit.
-   Treatment estimate is automatically calculated.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Decisions

1.  Medical alert?
2.  Need X-ray?
3.  Patient accepts treatment?
4.  Decline permanently?
5.  Start treatment today?
