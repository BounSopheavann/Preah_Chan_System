SCREEN NAVIGATION SPECIFICATION PLAN
Dental Clinic Management System
Version 1.0

PURPOSE
This document series defines how users navigate through the Dental Clinic Management System.
It complements the Business Flow, System Flow, and ERD documents. It does not replace them.

RECOMMENDED STRUCTURE

Instead of one huge document, split the navigation into six modules that match the existing
Business Flow and System Flow.

Navigation 1
Patient Registration & Appointment Booking
Screens:
- Login
- Dashboard
- Patient List
- New Patient
- Patient Profile
- Appointment Booking
- Appointment Detail
- Calendar

Navigation 2
Arrival & Check-in
Screens:
- Today's Appointments
- Check-in
- Waiting Queue
- Walk-in Registration

Navigation 3
Clinical Examination
Screens:
- Today's Queue
- Patient Summary
- Examination
- Odontogram
- X-ray Viewer
- Treatment Plan

Navigation 4
Treatment Execution
Screens:
- Treatment Execution
- Procedure Recording
- Clinical Images
- Prescription
- Treatment Summary

Navigation 5
Billing & Payment
Screens:
- Billing
- Invoice
- Payment
- Receipt
- Outstanding Balance

Navigation 6
Follow-up & Recall
Screens:
- Follow-up
- Recall
- Reminder Center
- Telegram Patient Portal
- Recall Dashboard

Each navigation document should describe:
- Purpose of every screen
- Who can access it
- Entry points
- Exit points
- Buttons and destinations
- Navigation after Save / Cancel / Delete
- Error navigation
- Related Business Flow
- Related System Flow
- Related ERD
- Role permissions

Estimated size:

Navigation 2: 4-5 pages

Navigation 4: 5-6 pages
Navigation 5: 5-6 pages
Navigation 6: 4-5 pages

Total:
6 TXT files

or developer to locate the relevant navigation specification for each module.
