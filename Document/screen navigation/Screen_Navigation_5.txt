SCREEN NAVIGATION 5
Dental Clinic Management System

MODULE
Billing, Payment & Invoice Processing

PURPOSE

Defines the navigation for billing, invoice generation, payment collection,
receipt generation and outstanding balance management.

Related Documents
- Business Flow 5
- System Flow 5
- ERD 5

============================================================
PRIMARY USERS
============================================================

Receptionist
Administrator

============================================================
SCREEN HIERARCHY
============================================================

Treatment Summary
      ↓
Billing
      ├── Invoice
      ├── Discount Approval
      ├── Payment
      ├── Receipt
      └── Transaction History

============================================================
BILLING
============================================================

Entry:
Treatment Summary → Finish Visit

Displays:
- Patient
- Completed procedures
- Charges
- Outstanding balance

Buttons:
Generate Invoice
View Invoice
Back

============================================================
INVOICE
============================================================

Displays:
- Invoice number
- Procedures
- Tooth numbers
- Unit prices
- Discounts
- Tax
- Grand total

Buttons:
Apply Discount
Receive Payment
Print Invoice
Back

Validation:
Invoice must contain at least one completed procedure.

============================================================
DISCOUNT APPROVAL
============================================================

Entry:
Invoice → Apply Discount

Fields:
- Discount type
- Value
- Reason

Decision:
Authorization required?

YES:
Request Admin Approval

NO:
Apply discount immediately

Buttons:
Approve
Reject
Cancel

============================================================
PAYMENT
============================================================

Methods:
- Cash
- Card
- Bank Transfer
- KHQR
- Mixed Payment

Fields:
- Amount
- Reference
- Notes

Buttons:
Complete Payment
Save Partial Payment
Cancel

Success:
Open Receipt Screen

============================================================
RECEIPT
============================================================

Displays:
- Receipt number
- Payment summary
- Remaining balance

Buttons:
Print
Send Telegram
Send Email
Finish

Finish:
Navigate to Follow-up (Navigation 6)

============================================================
TRANSACTION HISTORY
============================================================

Displays:
- Previous payments
- Refunds
- Adjustments

Buttons:
View Detail
Back

============================================================
GLOBAL RULES
============================================================

Save:
Remain on current screen.

Complete Payment:
Update invoice status.
Refresh patient balance.

Back:
Return to previous screen.

Logout:
Return to Login.

============================================================
ERROR NAVIGATION
============================================================

Payment exceeds balance:
Remain on Payment screen.

Authorization rejected:
Return to Invoice.

Invoice missing items:
Prevent payment processing.

END OF SCREEN NAVIGATION 5
